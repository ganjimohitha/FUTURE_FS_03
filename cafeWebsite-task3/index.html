Our Story

Born from Passion,
Brewed with Purpose

Noir & Bloom was founded by childhood friends Anika & Ravi, who quit their corporate careers in 2019 to pursue one simple obsession — a truly perfect cup of coffee in a space that felt like home.

Every bean we source is ethically traded, every pastry baked fresh each morning, and every corner of our café designed for you to slow down, breathe, and just be.
